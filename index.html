Welcome to the Sad Slut Club

What is this?

This is a website that I threw together so that I can have a place to just toss all my half finished projects for ease of linking them to people. It's easier to explore what I do when it's all in one place, anyway. I've also decided to document some aspects of my mental illness here, because I think it's especially useful for me to write a lot of it down.

The name Sad Slut Club was a joke that I took way too far and now I'm emotionally attached to this domain. Plus, .club domains are cheap as fuck.

Who are you?

I'm Selkie / Charlotte. I do a wide variety of things like art, tabletop nonsense, light gamedev, and writing. Selkie is a handle that I came up with in 2016 and it has stuck ever since. For my art, specifically, I use the username pantywitch or some variation thereof because I think it's cute, and Selkie is often taken.
